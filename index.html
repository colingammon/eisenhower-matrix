Eisenhower Matrix

Capture tasks, move them between urgency and importance, and keep your plan saved automatically.

Shortcuts: Ctrl/Cmd + Enter to add, Ctrl + Shift + 1–4 to quick-add, Ctrl/Cmd + F to search, click title or E to edit, Delete to remove, 1–4 to move, Ctrl/Cmd + Z to undo, Ctrl/Cmd + R to redo, Esc to exit, and ↑/↓ to reorder.

Note: On Mac, if Ctrl+Shift+2 doesn't work, use Ctrl+Shift+S to add to "Schedule" instead. See SHORTCUTS.md for all keyboard commands.
